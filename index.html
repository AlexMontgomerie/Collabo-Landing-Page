Who Are We?

We are a music collaboration startup with the focus of connecting local musicians with one another.

We are musicians ourselves and have a philosophy; when you put two musicians in a room together, give them enough time to be creative then good things will always follow. So we are creating a product to do exactly that.

As a musician you can find people to...
Record

Find musicians and promoters who want to record more and get yourself into the studio!

Jam

Find musicians with a similar niche to you. Meetup and jam your hearts out!

Gig

Find musicians and promoters who want to put on more live shows and start gigging.

Collab

Find musicians you want to work with on long term projects. Solidify your network.

A Music Network

Music is our passion and ethic.

We want to build a community for musicians to come to online - to find contacts also looking to connect and build a network locally. Connecting with musicians all over the globe is great but can be frustrating when you just want to meet up and play/create.

We want to provide a platform for upcoming musicians who want to seek out other people close to them and just want to create music and do something more with it. We’ve been throwing some live music nights in Belfast at The Bear and The Doll. Check out the highlights! If you’re an artist interested in performing then please contact us at partners@collabo.uk

Collabo's Story: Alex & Suraj

Hi guys my name is Suraj and I am one of the co-founders of Collabo alongside my university friend Alex. Alex and I met in 1st year while studying Electrical and Electronic Engineering at Imperial College London. We immediately connected over our love for music. While studying together we'd play numerous hip hop tracks from upcoming artists in London and before long we began regularly going out to music events all over the city!

Fast forward a year; Alex and I come up with this concept of a platform through which musicians can find each other in their local areas based on their interests and musical preferences. We both agreed that it was rather hard to make time to find musicians other musicians that we gelled well with!

Collabo is a personal venture for both of us as it is a platform that we would have found incredibly useful when we first started our music endeavours. Alex and I can both make music - I am a hip hop vocalist while Alex is a jazz guitarist. We've both been making music for years now. Alex is apart of multiple bands and has played at numerous venues throughout London over the years! I started making music by producing beats and writing rhymes at the age of 12 in Belfast. At that time not as many people were making hip hop in Belfast so finding connections was hard - if only I had Collabo!

Main Musician Features



Initially we will launch an MVP version of our platform with only newsfeed, profile and messaging features to get started. After this release we will gradually update the platform with the full range of features stated below. All users who sign up to our MVP will gain access the full list of premium features for free!

Profile

Fully customisable profiles with musician specific information. This feature will make it easy for musicians to be found by, and to find others with similar interests.

Newsfeed

A public newsfeed that will show posts from other musicians and businesses in the local area and boosted posts by relevant musicians/businesses e.g. if an artist is focused on recording they may see boosted posts from local studios.

Messaging

Users will be able to directly message one another with our in-house messaging service. Messages can be starred and notifications will be sent via email.

Collabo File Storage

Users will be able to upload and store files on the Collabo platform. This feature is so artists who collab with one another can host all the relevant project files in the same place and earn rewards for collaborating with other users.

Payment Services

We understand that making an income is an integral part to furthering one's music career. That is why this feature always users to charge for their music services through the Collabo platform. E.g. If you are a mix engineer and wish to charge for your services then you can make posts that are essentially ads or offers for your services.

Advanced Search Criteria

Users will be able to input highly specific search criteria to locate and find musicians near them that align with their current goals and interests. E.g. a user may search for saxaphone players, up-to 50km away, who are currently looking to jam and record more music.

Main Business Featrures



In the future we will be opening services to businesses such as studios, rehearsal spaces, music equipment companies and record labels. Below are some features that we will make available to businesses.

Local Customer Network

We will be giving businesses full access to our database of users who have opted in to getting offers and deals from businesses. The user base that the businesses will have access to will be highly specific and targeted. E.g. Rehearsal rooms will be able to find all the musicians in the city who are currently focused on jamming so they can market to them effectively.

Payment Services

We will provide in-house payment services to businesses who wish to attract customers via Collabo. This is so any advertising they do on Collabo can be immediately converted into a sale to the relevant musicians.

Smart Ads

Businesses who wish to focus on advertising their products and increasing their brand awareness among the cities musicians can pay for Collabo's "smart ad" service which uses the information we have in our database to create leads and sales for music businesses. By targeting relevant musicians we plan to increase customer retention for businesses.

As A Musician, You Can Expect

Music Network Suggestions
Profile and Messaging
Local Artist Locating
Community Newsfeed Filter
Portfolio Display and Storage
#1

Music Network Suggestions

Using our algorithm, we will suggest your profile based on your music needs and goals with other musicians and businesses near you prompting you to get in touch over Collabo.

Getting in contact with other artists and people like studios and rehearsal spaces is never a bad thing. This feature is for helping you expand your network.

#2

Profile and Messaging

Building your profile is how we understand what you are all about better as an artist. We will ask you your music stats such as what instruments you play, equiupment you use, are seeking paid gigs, how many hours a week on average and your current focuses (record, jam etc.).

Other users can visit your profile and this helps them find you through searches. If someone has a home studio and you are looking to record a single for your EP then you guys can easily find one another, talk via our messaging service and then form a link.

#3

Local Artist Locating.

Using the search bar you can find other musicians and businesses based off parameters you are looking for such as an electric guitarist or a rehearsal room owner.

We will help you find the right people to add to your network and progress your music goals.

#4

Community Newsfeed Filter.

The newsfeed is where you will find other musicians posting about projects, proposals and invitations to collaborate on.

The best part is it will be all the things happening around you. You can vary this with a location filter at the top of the newsfeed and only see posts of a certain kind if you want. This advanced filtering will save you a lot of time finding the exact people you want to connect with.

#5

Portfolio Display and Storage

On the profile page we will have a custom display for your music. You will be able to embed links from YouTube and SoundCloud but there's more.

You will also be able to initiate collaborations online with other artists after meeting up and post any relevant files to your collaboration over our platform and use it as a music cloud.
